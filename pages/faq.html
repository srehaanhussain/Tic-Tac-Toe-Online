Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I start playing?

To start playing, simply create an account or log in if you already have one. Once logged in, you'll be able to choose from different game modes: Player vs Player (Local), Player vs AI, or Player vs Player (Online).

What are the different game modes?

We offer three game modes:

  • Local Multiplayer: Play against a friend on the same device
  • AI Challenge: Test your skills against our intelligent computer opponent
  • Online Play: Challenge players from around the world

Gameplay

How do I make a move?

Making a move is simple! Just click or tap on any empty square in the 3x3 grid. The game will automatically place your symbol (X or O) in the selected square.

How do I win?

To win, you need to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game will automatically detect a win and declare the winner.

What happens in case of a tie?

If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is declared a draw. You can start a new game by clicking the "New Game" button.

Online Play

How do I play online?

To play online, select the "Player vs Player (Online)" option from the game modes. You'll be matched with another player looking for a game. Once matched, you can start playing!

Can I play with friends?

Yes! You can create a private game room and share the room code with your friend. They can join using the same code to play against you.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard tracks your performance in online games. You earn points for wins and can see how you rank against other players globally.

Account & Settings

How do I change my username?

You can change your username in the account settings section. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Account Settings" to make changes.

Can I reset my stats?

Yes, you can reset your game statistics at any time. Go to your account settings and click on "Reset Stats" to clear your game history and start fresh.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

Our application works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure you're using the latest version for the best experience.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our web application is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices through your browser.

Need more help?

If you have any other questions or need assistance, please contact us through our social media channels or email support.
