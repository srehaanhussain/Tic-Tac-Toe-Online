Online Play Guide

Take your Tic Tac Toe skills online and compete with players from around the world. This guide will help you navigate our online features and make the most of your multiplayer experience.

Getting Started with Online Play

1. Quick Match

Jump into a game instantly by clicking "Quick Match". Our system will automatically pair you with another player of similar skill level.

2. Private Rooms

Create a private room to play with friends. Share the room code with them, and they can join your game.

3. Room Browser

Browse available public rooms and join games that interest you. Filter by player count, skill level, or game settings.

Online Features

1. Chat System

Communicate with other players using our in-game chat. Send quick messages or use emojis to express yourself during matches.

2. Leaderboards

Track your progress and compete for the top spot on our global and regional leaderboards. Earn points for wins and maintain your ranking.

3. Match History

Review your past matches, analyze your performance, and learn from your gameplay. Track your win/loss ratio and improvement over time.

Online Play Tips

  • Check your internet connection before starting a match
  • Use the chat system respectfully and follow community guidelines
  • Take advantage of the match history to improve your skills
  • Try different strategies against various opponents
  • Report any inappropriate behavior through our support system

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

If you experience connection problems, try refreshing your browser or checking your internet connection. Our system will attempt to reconnect you to your game.

Game Disconnections

In case of a disconnection, you have a short window to reconnect to your game. If you can't reconnect, the game will be recorded as a loss.

Reporting Problems

If you encounter any issues with online play, use our support system to report them. Include details about the problem and any error messages you see.
