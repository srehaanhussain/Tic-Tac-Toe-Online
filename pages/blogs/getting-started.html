Getting Started with Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to our Tic Tac Toe application! This guide will help you get started with all the features and game modes available.

Creating Your Account

To get started, you'll need to create an account:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Choose a unique username
  4. Click "Create Account" to complete the process

Game Modes

Our application offers three exciting game modes:

Local Multiplayer

Play against a friend on the same device. Perfect for quick games and family fun!

AI Challenge

Test your skills against our intelligent AI opponent. Choose from different difficulty levels.

Online Play

Challenge players from around the world. Create private rooms to play with friends.

How to Play

The rules of Tic Tac Toe are simple:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid
  • Players take turns marking a space with their symbol (X or O)
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all spaces are filled without a winner, the game is a draw

Features and Settings

Customize your gaming experience:

  • Change your username and profile picture
  • Adjust sound effects and music volume
  • Enable/disable animations
  • View your game statistics and achievements

Tips for Beginners

Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Start with the center position for better winning chances
  • Watch for your opponent's potential winning moves
  • Practice against the AI to improve your skills
  • Try different strategies in local multiplayer mode
