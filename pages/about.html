About Us

Creator

This Tic Tac Toe application was created by Rehaan Hussain, a passionate developer and entrepreneur dedicated to creating engaging and innovative digital experiences.

Our Mission

This Tic Tac Toe application was built with the vision of bringing people together through classic gaming experiences. We believe in creating simple yet engaging platforms that connect people across the globe. Our goal is to provide a seamless gaming experience that combines the nostalgia of traditional games with modern technology and design.

The application features multiple game modes, including local multiplayer, AI challenges, and online play, making it accessible and enjoyable for players of all ages and skill levels.
